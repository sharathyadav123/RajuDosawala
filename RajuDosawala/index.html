About us

Some words about us

Raju Dosawala Caterers was started in 1984, through out the decades we have reputed ourselves as traditional South Indian Caterers to deliver authentic taste.

We are here to make your special moments even more special. Whether you are planning for a great Wedding or Kitty parties or any other functions like- Sangeet, Mehendi, Engagement, Birthday Party, In-House parties, Farewell or any others event nationally or internationally, We'll be always there to serve you as per your needs and likes..

Our special team gives it's best for your occasions, thus making your occasion even more special. Please refer our online Menu, we have modernised it keeping in mind the authenticity & taste of our historic tradition. Our Special clientele list includes Celebrities and many business organizations. So call us now & we'll make sure your event is memorable.
